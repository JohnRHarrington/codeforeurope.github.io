We're on a mission to make government
work in the digital age.

Code for Europe is a network of developers and designers that create digital services for cities and communities.

We do this in order to solve real, human problems in a reusable, flexible and inclusive way. We empower local communities to build, share and adapt civic tech. We solve local challenges by profiting from the shared experience of an international network of likeminded fellows.

For cities and other governments

Coders can help cities collaborate in bottom-up innovation. Code for Europe promotes and facilitates development of software solutions together with governments. We connect people to share knowledge, experience and code between cities.

In-house fellowships

Having a small team of developers and designers that working within government, finding problems, creating solutions and helping spread awareness of how contemporary technology works among the government workforce.

Supporting brigades

Having an active community of coders working on apps and data services to support the efforts of your local government.
